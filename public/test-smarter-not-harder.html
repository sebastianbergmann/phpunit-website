Test Smarter, Not Harder

Live Online Zoom Session on February 7, 2025

A new major version of PHPUnit is released every year on the first Friday in February. On the same day, The PHP Consulting Company (thePHP.cc) hosts a live online session that is free to attend for everyone:

"PHPUnit 12: Test Smarter, not Harder" on February 7, 2025 at 10:00 (Europe/Berlin)

You can no longer register for this event because it has already happened.

In this live online Zoom session, Sebastian Bergmann, the creator and maintainer of PHPUnit, will share unique insights on how to get the most out of PHPUnit in 2025.

This includes the use of attributes instead of annotations, a significantly improved handling of deprecations, warnings and notices that no longer automatically lead to failing tests, better options for capturing code coverage, and a clean separation between stubs and mocks, which has been prepared behind the scenes for years.

Attendance is free. The session takes place on Friday, February 7, 2025, the release date of PHPUnit 12, from 10am until noon (CET).
